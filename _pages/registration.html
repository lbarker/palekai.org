Good to Know

  • Your membership is good for through the end of February 2027.
  • Dues for 2026 are $390 ($200 for full-time students).

Complete the following steps to sign up for a great year of paddling with Pale Kai Outrigger!

#1: Create (or Update) your SCORA Account

Go to https://www.scora.org/login/. New paddlers will need to create a free SCORA account. Make a note of your SCORA ID. Once your SCORA registration is complete, you will be sent to Smartwaiver to sign your waiver.

Returning paddlers should ensure their contact info is correct and sign the Smartwaiver. (Note: If you've already signed the Smartwaiver this calendar year, you won't be given the option to sign another. That's okay, just move on.)

Be aware that when prompted for your SCORA ID in Smartwaiver you MUST include the SC prefix.

After completing your Smartwaiver you'll be emailed a link to your completed Smartwaiver PDF. We recommend that you download and save it; the link is only good for three days and you may be asked for it later.

#2: Annual PKO Member Registration

  • And relax... because you just signed up for a great year of paddling!
